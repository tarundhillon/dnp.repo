Requirements Management

Requirements gathering phase is one of the most important phases for a programme. Poorly capctured requirements, leads to poor design and delays to build phase, but more curcially it often brings a constant stream of change requests which are difficult and expensive to incorporate. At D&P our ability to deal with unstructured data allows us to integrate with offline requirements document or standard requirement management products. We are able to visualise requirements during the capcture stage which enables the team to conduct indept analysis for quality, consistency and coverage.
